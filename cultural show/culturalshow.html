Cultural Show

It’s not only about the food anymore. We serve our guests with Traditional Nepali Cultural dance and folk music including various other cultural shows which represents more than 100 distinct ethnic groups of Nepal from different communities. Lakhe dance, Maithili dance, Newari dance and the Tharu cultural dance including an artist performing a peacock dance in a peacock costume are few of the most mind blowing performance you can see live at NEPALI CHULO. You can enjoy the live Nepali folk songs and many more other cultural music performed by various artists during your dinner time. At the same time we are proud to say that we have created a positive impact on preserving our Traditional Nepali Cultural dance & music. We heartily welcome you to NEPALI CHULO and we promise to make your visit an amazing experience of your life.
