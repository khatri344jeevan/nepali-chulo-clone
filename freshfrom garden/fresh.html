Fresh From Garden

We are very much concerned in the terms of food & hygiene as we believe in” GOOD FOOD FOR GOOD LIFE”. We understand the significant values of fresh & healthy food. So every day we get fresh fruits and vegetables in our kitchen. We do not compromise with the health of our valuable guests.
